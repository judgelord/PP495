In sum

Most Republicans support:

  • Schools teaching about global warming
  • US participation in Paris Agreement
  • Funding research for wind and solar
  • Regulating CO2
  • Tax credits for electrification
  • Using more renewables, less fossil fuels
  • Solar and wind on public lands

Most Republicans oppose:

  • Blocking offshore wind farms
  • Ordering agencies to stop researching global warming
  • Ordering agencies to stop providing info on global warming to the public
  • Eliminating FEMA

Most people in Texas, Arizona, New Mexico, Florida, Nevada, North Carolina, South Carolina, Georgia (and most other states) believe that climate change is already causing

  • Extreme heat
  • Wildfires
  • Flooding
  • Hurricanes
  • Drought AND
  • Rising sea levels

(only in WV and ND do majorities not think climate change is already having an impact)
